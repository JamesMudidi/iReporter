Intervention Report

Kisoro District Land Slide

Over 1,000 households have been destroyed and 2, 000 people displaced from their houses in five sub counties of Kisoro district from landslides and floods that have hit the area. The major sub counties affected included Nyarubuye, Kanaba, Murora, Nyabwishenya and Busanza. This rainfall that was characterized by severe strong wind and hail stones left two secondary schools, over 50 families a church and many others affected in the above mentioned sub counties.
